About Us

To establish 3Way Automation & Services as the preferred provider of Industrial Automation Solutions among both existing and potential customers. To deliver optimal solutions and maintain exceptional pre-sale and post-sale support, adhering to our high standards and customer requirements. To achieve annual growth in our market presence by consistently meeting and exceeding customer expectations.

3Way Automation & Services is dedicated to designing and manufacturing high-quality control products that integrate electrical, electronic, and automation components. Our team leverages the latest design software and tools, supported by in-house testing facilities to ensure the quality and functionality of our control products. Our solutions are utilized across various industries, including pump panels, car parking projects, process control, machine tools, textiles, engineering, and home automation.
